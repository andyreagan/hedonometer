Methodology

To analyze each book, we first break down the full text into 1,000 word segments. From there, a timeseries is created by joining 10 segments to create a window containing 10,000 words, and sliding this window across the book.

The dashed lines at the start and end of the timeseries correspond to the non-symmetric window, where only the right/left half was available.
