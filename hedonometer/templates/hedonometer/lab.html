Average Happiness for Twitter: Labs

Attempting to build a fine grained tool for sentiment analysis on Twitter. Key features are (1) variable time range, (2) variable time resolution, (3) variable range shift selection and (4) variable lens. The time range should be both visually (on the timeseries) and manually edit-able (calendars and such). For starters, just manually. Building an API to respond to arbitrarily time ranges with any resolution will be the most important feature. The time resolution will be chosen for you, based on the size of the time range. For very narrow ranges, we hope to provide minute-level resolution, and for the widest ranges, day resolution. The shift range selection will also be both visually and manually controlled. Or maybe just manually...multiple brushes on the same svg are tricky. This will be a lot like the existing books app.
